Registering a Late Arrival

A new player can join a tournament already in progress. They will be placed randomly in an available walkover spot in Frontside Round 1 and participate as a fully registered, paid player from that point forward.

To register a late arrival, enable the Developer Console in Global Settings, then open it by clicking the version number in the lower-right corner of the Tournament Bracket.
